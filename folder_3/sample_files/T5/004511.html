About Us
Whether you are enjoying the world-famous hot springs or climbing the vertical ice in the Ouray ice park, OCEMS is ready to assist in an emergency.

Unique Challenges to OCEMS

While many ambulance services provide response only in the urban area, OCEMS provides EMS in a variety of situations and locations.  The OCEMS response district encompasses the northern side of Red Mountain Pass, also known as the "Million Dollar Highway."  This winding mountain road is one of the most challenging and beautiful drives in Southwest Colorado.  OCEMS also provides emergency response to the east side of Dallas Divide, the west side of Engineer Pass, Imogene Pass, and numerous other back country roads and trails.  Mt. Sneffels, a "14'er" is also within the boundaries of our response area.

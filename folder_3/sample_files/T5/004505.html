Alibi Bar- Interior Ceiling Collapse

WHAT:         Alibi Bar- Interior Ceiling Collapse

 WHEN:           Wednesday, October 8, 2008 

 WHERE:        1313 West El Camino Real, Mountain View, CA 

 DETAILS :

At 11:54 a.m. on Wednesday, October 8, 2008, the Mountain View Fire Department responded to a 911 call reporting a ceiling collapse, gas leak, and report of a person trapped at 1313 West El Camino Real. Upon arrival firefighters found that the interior ceiling of the business had collapsed, trapping one male who had alredy extricated himself from the debris.  He was treated for minor injuries on scene and was released.  Firefighters performed a primary search of the business to determine that there were no other victims trapped, evacuated the adjoining businesses, checked for gas leaks, and controlled the utilities to the business.  Firefighters then worked to ensure that surrounding businesses has not been affected by the collapse and that the area was safe and clear of hazards. 

Building Inspectors are on scene evaluating the structual integrity of the building.  Pending the full evaluation, the building has been temporarily yellow tagged by building officials and must remain closed.  No damage was reported to any of the adjacant businesses.

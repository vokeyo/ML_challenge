Converting Between OpLog Entries and Trouble Report Codes


Notes

  • These conversions (with the exception of Controls/Hardware and Controls/Software) are based purely on other operators' selections from Run 7. Don't use them blindly!
  • Remember that if an expert is called in, they must not go home after a Trouble-Report-drawing issue has been resolved, until they have explained to MCR what happened, how they resolved it and what to look out for in the future (if relevant).
  • One method for choosing your Trouble Report Code (TRC) is as follows:
    • Find out which group fixed the problem. Find the TRC that matches up with that group.
    • If different groups we working on it, see if they have a common responsibility within the TRC listings.
    • If the groups do not have common responsibilities, look at the underlying problem. Most likely the other groups were dealing with consequences of the problem, rather than the source itself. Look for the TRC that matches with the group that dealt with the underlying problem.
    • If you're still not sure, speak with the group that worked on the problem.
    • If a group was not called in, ask the OC to explain his or her understanding of the situation.
    • Failing all of this, email/call the MCR Group Leader and wait for instructions.
  • Of course, you can also look at the listings below, but they're no substitute for really understanding what's going on, and who owns what.

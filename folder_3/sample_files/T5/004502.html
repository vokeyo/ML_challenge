Vehicle Crash Shuts Down Kendall Drive


(Miami-Dade County, FL) -- On Tuesday, February 5, 2008, at 9:45 p.m., Miami-Dade Fire Rescue (MDFR) responded to a multi vehicle accident on Southwest 122nd Avenue and Kendall Drive.

When MDFR crews arrived, they encountered two cars heavily damaged as a result of a t-bone collision.  Fortunately, an off duty Miami Beach Police Officer, who was on her way home from work, discovered the accident and rendered assistance to the victims prior to the arrival of MDFR crews.  Firefighters quickly assessed the scene and found that the drivers of both vehicles were seriously injured.  The 23 year old female driver of one of the cars was extricated using the “Jaws of Life” and was transported via Air Rescue to the trauma center.  The other driver, a male in his 30’s, was also transported to the trauma center by ground.  Three other patients involved in the accident were treated and released on scene.  No other injuries were reported.

A total of 8 MDFR units including one Air Rescue Helicopter responded to the scene.  The cause of the accident is unknown and is currently under investigation by the Miami-Dade Police Department.

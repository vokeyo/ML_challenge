Senate 1426: Relating to Municipalities/Per Diem & Travel 

S1426    GENERAL BILL/CS/4TH ENG by Governmental Oversight and Productivity;
  Posey; (CO-SPONSORS) Dawson  (Compare H 1335, H 1493, CS/2ND ENG/S 2672)
  Municipalities/Per Diem & Travel; authorizes municipalities & agencies
  thereof to adopt per diem & travel expense policies for travelers,
  notwithstanding certain provision; provides for applicability of certain
  provision, if per diem & travel expense policies are or are not adopted;
  provides that counties, county officers, district school boards, &
  certain special districts may increase specified rates, etc. Amends
  166.021, 112.061.  EFFECTIVE DATE: 06/10/2003.

Alameda Depot Civic Association MLK Day Food Drive
Posted By: ADCA Alameda Depot Civic Association
Description:

We will be collecting dry and canned goods (no outdate items please) on Martin Luther King Jr. Day at our monthly meeting which is 5:30 - 6:30 PM at the WIA building at the corner of Reymond and Court in the Alameda Depot Historic District across from Pioneer Womens Park.

If you need assistance in getting the food to us on or before that night please call me at (575) 993-6521. We can pick the contributions up or you can drop them off at 215 W Greening.

Monty Sarvo - Executive Director of ADCA

Dates: Start:   Wednesday, January 07, 2009        End:   Tuesday, January 20, 2009

Days:             

Location: 
215 W Greening Ave, Las Cruces, NM, 88005 See Map
drive on Alameda Blvd to Las Cruces Ave turn West toward the Railroad Museum at the Train Depot and away from the Organ Mountains. Turn North (right) and park anywhere along that block. The WIA building is on the next corner on the East (right) side.

Tasks for Volunteers:  Bring food in the form of dry or canned goods. We will take the food to the soup kitchen the next day. Ask your neighbors, family and friends to give you something to contribute and to come to the meeting if they would like. All civic minded people are welcome.

Contact:
Name: Monty Sarvo
Email: president@alamedadepot.org
Phone: (575) 993-6521

Details:
Will lunch be provided for volunteers?  No
Will child care be provided?  No
Is this Opportunity family-friendly?  Yes

Age Groups Accepted: 

Type: Collections (food, clothing, etc)

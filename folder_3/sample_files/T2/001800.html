Marquette Asset Mapping Regional Forum

Contact:  Bonita Smith 517-335-5177
Agency: Michigan Regional Skills Alliances


Thursday November 8, 2007  9:00am - 4:00pm

Marquette Service Center
1498 O' Dovero Dr.
Marquette, Michigan 49855

As a follow up to our Asset Mapping Learning Institute on June 7, 2007, the Michigan Regional Skills Alliances are holding Asset Mapping Regional Forums.

The Michigan Regional Skills Alliances and the Department of Labor & Economic Growth/ Bureau of Workforce Programs invites you to an Asset Mapping Regional Forum. Nationally recognized facilitator and speaker, Lisa Hadden from Healthy Community Partners and St. Mary's of Michigan will be teaching the basics of Asset Mapping.

For more information, please see the documents below or call (517) 335-5858 or email RSA@Michigan.gov

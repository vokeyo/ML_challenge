Errol Heights Wetlands work day

Saturday, February 21, 2009
9:00 am to 12:00 pm

Help restore this wetland in the heart of Southeast Portland. Remove invasive plants like English ivy, Himalayan blackberry and clematis. Bring gloves and meet at the corner of Southeast 52nd and Tenino.

Registration and cost: To register, call 503-823-6131.

Errol Heights Wetlands

Southeast 52nd Avenue and Tenino Court, Portland

Sponsored by Portland Parks and Recreation.

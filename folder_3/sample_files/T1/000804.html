HB 105                                             LC 26 0895 
 
 
 
 
 
 
                        A BILL TO BE ENTITLED 
                               AN ACT 
 
 
  1- 1  To amend Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, 
  1- 2  relating to crimes and offenses, so as to change certain 
  1- 3  definitions relating to use of force in the defense of 
  1- 4  habitations and property; to repeal conflicting laws; and 
  1- 5  for other purposes. 
 
  1- 6       BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF GEORGIA: 
 
  1- 7                           SECTION 1. 
 
  1- 8  Title 16 of the Official Code of Georgia Annotated, relating 
  1- 9  to crimes and offenses, is amended by striking in its 
  1-10  entirety Code Section 16-3-24.1, relating to habitation and 
  1-11  personal property defined, and inserting in lieu thereof a 
  1-12  new Code Section 16-3-24.1 to read as follows: 
 
  1-13    "16-3-24.1. 
 
  1-14    As used in Code Sections 16-3-23 and 16-3-24, the term 
  1-15    'habitation' means any dwelling, motor vehicle, or place 
  1-16    of business, or property owned, leased, or under the legal 
  1-17    control of a person using force in its defense, and 
  1-18    'personal property' means personal property other than a 
  1-19    motor vehicle." 
 
  1-20                           SECTION 2. 
 
  1-21  All laws and parts of laws in conflict with this Act are 
  1-22  repealed.

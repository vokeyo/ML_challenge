Short Tale Contest

Young readers created! The Chattanooga - Hamilton County Bicentennial Library sponsored a short tale contest in celebration of the 2005/2006 Tale for One City book, Watsons Go to Birmingham - 1963 by Christopher Paul Curtis. Children and young adults were to write a short tale about a life changing event. The contest ended January 14, 2006 and the judging took place the last week in January. A 1st place and a runner-up were chosen in every grade that had entries. Each 1st place winner received a $25 gift certificate from the Bicentennial Library Bookstore and each runner-up received a $10 certificate.

The library would like to give special thanks to members of the Chattanooga Writer’s Guild for judging our Essay Contest this year. http://www.chattanoogawritersguild.org/

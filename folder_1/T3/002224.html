    Title Gell-Mann{endash}Low equation: Relation between the sixth-order vacuum polarization coefficients
    Creator/Author Nigam, B.P. [Department of Physics& Astronomy, Box 871504, Arizona State University, Tempe, Arizona 85287-1504 (United States)]
    Publication Date1996 Oct 01
    OSTI IdentifierOSTI ID: 385877
    Other Number(s)PRVDAQ; ISSN 0556-2821
    Resource TypeJournal Article
    Resource RelationPhysical Review, D ; VOL. 54 ; ISSUE: 8 ; PBD: Oct 1996
    Subject66 PHYSICS ; QUANTUM ELECTRODYNAMICS; LOW EQUATION; VACUUM POLARIZATION; GELL-MANN THEORY; PHOTONS; PROPAGATOR; EXPANSION

    Description/Abstract Gorishny, Kataev, Larin, and Surguladze have obtained the fourth-order Gell-Mann{endash}Low function{psi}{sub 4} and the Callan-Symanzik function{beta}{sub 4}. In an earlier paper, we have used their result to establish a relationship between the fifth-order vacuum polarization coefficient{ital b}{sub 5} and the fifth-order Gell-Mann{endash}Low function{psi}{sub 5}. In this paper, the relationship between the sixth-order{ital b}{sub 6} and{psi}{sub 6} is obtained using the Gell-Mann{endash}Low equation and other relations for the Gell-Mann{endash}Low function.{copyright}{ital 1996 The American Physical Society.}

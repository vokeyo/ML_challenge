R/V C. Magister
Vessel Specifications


R/V C. Magister at Spud Point Marina
Credit: Michael Carver

Length Overall: 33 ft.
Beam: 11 ft.
Displacement: 9 ft.
Gross Tons: 8
Built: in 2001 in Nova Scotia, Canada
Designed by: Gaski Boats of Iceland
Propulsion: Twin 205 hp Volvo Diesel engines linked to Volvo Penta Out drives
Cruising Speed: 15 knots
Top Speed: 18 knots
Ship Power: One 2.5 KW Pro-sign Inverter with 4 8D gell cell batteries
Fuel Capacity: 300 imperial gal.
Fuel Consumption: 15 gal./hr. at standard cruising speed. (Diesel)
Potable Water Capacity: 100 gal.
1 Head with holding tank
Safety Equipment: 6 Person Life raft, 6 life jackets with strobes and whistles, 1 GPS equipped EPIRB, VHF Radio w/DSC
Personnel: 1 Captain

Deck Equipment:
1. Aluminum Aframe rated @ 1000lbs safe working load
2. Aluminum Davit rated @ 500lbs safe working load
3. Aluminum acoustic arm
4. Kolstrand hydraulic winch with 3/16th conducting cable
5. Observation Platform with 5' railing and three seats and laptop mount with power

Electronics: Fully equipped with Simrad C41 Radar, WASS GPS, Simrad DSC equipped VHF radio, Simard autohelm with Robertson AP20 and gyrocompass, Laptop with Visual Navigation Suite, Simrad EK60 Sounder with 120kHz transducer, SBE 45 Micro TSG Thermosalinograph with data logger.

Work Deck Space: 80 ft.2 open main deck, aft of House

Cargo Holds: Over 50 ft.3 available for gear stowage.

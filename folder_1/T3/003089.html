Travel the world without leaving your own backyard at the City World Market.  You’ll find dozens of artisans and crafters from around the globe, displaying one-of-a-kind treasures from jewelry and baskets to hand woven fabrics and works of art.

The artists featured below participated in a unique "artisan to entrepreneur" program this past summer.  In collaboration with the Montgomery College Hispanic Business and Training Institute, these crafters received marketing and business education to help them become entrepreneurs in their adopted country.

• City World Market Receives Maryland Economic Development Association Award

• Gaithersburg Receives National Cultural Diversity Award

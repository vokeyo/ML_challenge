  • TAB P - Petition for special exception use pursuant to section 29-4, schedule I and section 29-9(25) of the City Code of Ordinances to allow an unattached accessory structure in the rear-yard setback in the R-1 District, Ricardo Costa-13250 Biscayme Bay Terrace (V-47-07)

Data Sets

Hemiptera

Cicadomorpha

Reference:
Cryan, J.R. In Press. Molecular Phylogeny of Cicadomorpha (Insecta: Hemiptera: Cicadoidea, Cercopoidea, and Membracoidea): Adding Evidence to the Controversy. Systematic Entomology.
Data Set:
Aligned partial nucleotide sequences from 18S rDNA, 28S rDNA, and Histone 3
Notes:
Individual sequences available on Genbank; see reference for accession numbers. Blocked alignment matric and published phylogeny available on TreeBASE; see reference for accession numbers. Sequencher files available upon request.
Cryan In Press Data Set (nexus format)


Membracoidea
Reference:
Cryan, J.R., B.M. Wiegmann, L.L. Deitz, and C.H. Dietrich. 2000. Phylogeny of the Treehoppers (Insecta: Hempitera: Membracidae): Evidence from Two Nuclear Genes. Molecular Phylogenetics and Evolution 17(2): 317-334
Data Set:
Aligned partial nucleotide sequences from EF-1a and 28S rDNA
Notes:
Individual sequences available on Genbank; see reference for accession numbers. Sequencher files available upon request.
Cryan et al. 2000 Data Set (nexus format)


Reference:
Cryan, J.R., J.A. Robertson, and L.L. Deitz. 2003. The New World Treehopper Tribe Microcentrini (Hempitera: Membracidae: Stegaspidinae): Monographic Revision and Phylogenetic Position. Thomas Say Publications in Entomology: Monographs of the Entomological Society of America
Data Set:
63 morphological features of the adult and nymphal integument and male female terminalia
Notes:
See reference for character/code descriptions.
Cryan et al. 2003 Data Set (nexus format)


Reference:
Cryan, J.R., M.F. Whiting, L.L. Deitz, B.M. Wiegmann, C.H. Dietrich, and S.H. McKamey. 2004. Treehopper Trees: Phylogeny of Membracidae (Hempitera: Cicadomorpha: Membracoidea): Based on Molecules and Morphology. Systematic Entomology 29: 441-454.
Data Set:
Aligned partial nucleotide sequences from EF-1a, Wg, 18S rDNA, and 28S rDNA and 83 characters of adult and nymphal morphology
Notes:
Individual sequences available on Genbank; see reference for accession numbers. Data matrix and published phylogeny available on TreeBASE; see reference for accession numbers. Sequencher files available upon request.
Cryan et al. 2004 Data Set (nexus format)

Carabidae

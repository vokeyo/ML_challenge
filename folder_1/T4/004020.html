September 25, 2008
Governor Kulongoski Announces Executive Appointments
 
(Salem)– Governor Kulongoski today announced the Oregon Senate confirmed the appointment of the following list of nominees to Oregon boards and commissions.
 
“Serving on a board or commission is an excellent way for citizens to get directly involved with their government,” said Governor Kulongoski. “This list of appointments is another example of Oregonians selflessly serving this state for the common good of all of us.”

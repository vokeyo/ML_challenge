Learn more about Gerald Ford and other United States Presidents by reading books included in this bibliography.

Selected books for kids about the Presidents, First Ladies and their children.

Aaron, Jan. Gerald R. Ford: President of Destiny. Fleet, 1975.

Ada, David R. Gerald R. Ford, 38th President of the United States. Fort Collins: Garrett Educational Corp., 1990.

Anderson, La Vere. Tad Lincoln, Abe's Son. Champaign: Garrard Pub. Co., 1971.

Biographies of the First Ladies of the United States. Videotape. Exec. Prod. William Cole Smith. Prod. Phoenix Multimedia & Duplication. Huntsville: Phoenix Multimedia, 1998.

Bourne, Miriam Anne. White House Children. New York: Random House, 1979.

Gilleo, Alma. Amy Carter, Growing Up in the White House. Elgin: Child's World, 1978.

Hoyt, Edwin Palmer. Grover Cleveland. Chicago: Reilly & Lee Co., 1962.

Clinton Maloney. The First Ladies. Children's Press 1994.

Gormley, Beatrice. First Ladies: Women who called the White House home. Scholastic Inc. 1996.

Mercer, Charles E. Gerald Ford. New York: Putnam, 1975.

Randolph, Sallie G. Gerald R. Ford, President. New York: Walker, 1987.

Santow, Dan. Jacqueline Bouvier Kennedy Onassis 1929-1994. Children's Press 1998.

Sipiera, Paul P. Gerald R. Ford: Thirty-Eighth President of the United States. Chicago: Children's Press, 1989.

Weil, Anne. Martha Washington. Simon and Schuster 1989.

___ Anne. Abigail Adams. Simon and Schuster 1989.

___ Anne. Eleanor Roosevelt. Simon and Schuster 1989.


Books about Grand Rapids, MI

Olson, Gordon L. A Grand Rapids Sampler. Grand Rapids: The Grand Rapids Historical Commission, 1992.

Elliot, Gerald. Grand Rapids: Renaissance on the Grand. Tulsa: Continental Heritage Press, 1982.

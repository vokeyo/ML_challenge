    Title COSMOTRON INJECTION SYSTEM ENGINEERING AND OPERATION NOTES--PULSED LINER, ANGLE CORRECTOR AND INFLECTOR PULSING SYSTEMS
    Creator/Author Turner, C.M.
    Publication Date1961 May 25
    OSTI IdentifierOSTI ID: 4836115
    Report Number(s)BNL-5751
    Resource TypeTechnical Report
    Research OrgBrookhaven National Lab., Upton, N.Y.
    SubjectPHYSICS; CIRCUITS; CONFIGURATION; CONTROL SYSTEMS; COSMOTRONS; ELECTRIC POTENTIAL; ELECTRONIC EQUIPMENT; ENERGY; INJECTION; MAGNETIC FIELDS; OPERATION; OPTICAL SYSTEMS; PLANNING; PROTON BEAMS; PULSES; SYNCHROTRONS; VARIATIONS
    Description/Abstract The pulsed liner, angle corrector, and inflector pulsing systems of the cosmotron injection system are described. Compensation of terminal voltage change, increasing proton energy to match dH/dt, liner geometry and constants, compensation for curvature of liner waveform, angle corrector and inflector pulse delay, waveform observing circuits, and system setup and adjustment are discussed. (M.C.G.)

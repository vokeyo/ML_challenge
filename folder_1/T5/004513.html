News Release 06/037/0451/SB/(1)

February 6, 2006

 

 

Man Charged in School Bus Accident

 

            A 45-year-old man has been charged in an early morning accident involving a Fairfax County school bus.  At 7 a.m. on Monday, February 6, the Fairfax County school bus was traveling east on Braddock Road approaching Sully Road.  At the same time, a Metrobus was traveling west on Braddock Road when it collided with the school bus.  Officers at the scene determined that the Metrobus crossed over the double-yellow line and into the path of the school bus.  As a result, both buses impacted on the driver’s side and the school bus driver was trapped inside the bus. 

 

            Fire and Rescue personnel extricated the driver, a 26-year-old Manassas area woman and medevaced her to Inova Fairfax Hospital.  One student and a 67-year-old aid were transported by ground to Inova Fair Oaks Hospital. 

 

            The driver of the Metrobus, 45-year-old Wilbur H. Lucas, Jr., of 9906 Quiet Glen Court in Springdale, Maryland, was also transported by ground to Inova Fair Oaks Hospital and was later charged with reckless driving.

Pilates Arc (Spine Corrector)

The Pilates Arctm, also called Spine Corrector or Step Barrel, is an integral piece of equipment within the Pilates Method.  Unique movements that will enhance your overall fitness can be performed using this innovative equipment.  Mat exercises are combined with this apparatus which incorporate resistence, control, precision and flow.

Arc/Mat Combo - Intro

(Ages 14+) Introduction to the Pilates Arc, an effective Pilates apparatus, combined with Pilates mat exercises.  Pilates Method principles; concentration, precision, and control will be emphasized in this class which is designed to both challenge those who are familiar with Pilates and introduce those who would like to try something new and effective for their workout routine.

 

Make-up policy for Pilates Arc Classes:

Due to limited equipment, students who miss a Pilates Arc class may attend a Pilates Mat class as a make-up, another Pilates Arc class if room is available, or purchase a private Pilates Reformer lesson for an additional fee of $40. Make-ups are arranged through your instructor.

No discounts, visitation, or punch card entry.

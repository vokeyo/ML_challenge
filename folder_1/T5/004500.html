    Subject: 8-20-07 CAR CRASH
    Summary: DANVILLE POLICE, FIRE, AND RESCUE UNITS REPONDED TO A CRASH ON PINEY FOREST ROAD THIS MORNING

    This morning at approximately 7:09 A.M. Danville Police, Fire, and Rescue units responded to a reported vehicle crash at the intersection of Piney Forest Road and Nelson Avenue. The subsequent investigation of the crash revealed the driver in this single vehicle accident to be Rafael Hernandez. Hernandez was driving northbound on Piney Forest Road when he lost control of his 1993 Dodge pick up truck, crossed the southbound lanes of traffic, and struck a utility pole. Hernandez had to extricated from his vehicle and transported to the Danville Regional Medical Center where he is listed in stable condition.

Willis new MITT manager

eggy Willis began work Feb. 15 as manager of the Transit Division's management information and transit technology section. She has shared responsibility as acting manager since the departure of Nancy Neuerburg last fall.

Willis came to Metro in 1986 as a research associate. She became supervisor of market strategy and evaluation in 1990, and supervisor of research and management information in 1996.

Before to coming to Metro she worked for the City of Seattle. She has also taught at Pacific Lutheran University and holds advanced degrees in sociology and research methods.

"Peggy has demonstrated leadership in her field," said Rick Walsh, Metro's general manager. "She is strongly committed to customer service and has expressed a great deal of enthusiasm about having an opportunity to implement her vision for the section's future."

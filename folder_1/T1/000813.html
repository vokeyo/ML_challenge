Burglary of a Habitation

#08-011773

 

On Monday, September 22, 2008 at approximately 11:08 AM, officers responded to a burglary of a habitation in progress in the 3300 block of Wildrye.  Upon arrival, officers observed two individuals fleeing the area on foot.  A foot pursuit ensued that resulted in the arrest of two suspects.  Further investigation revealed that the two forced entry into a home, removed property and fled.

 

Arrested and charged with evading arrest or detention and burglary of a habitation were:

 

Cameron Krumrey

17 year old

College Station resident

 

Alexander McCain

18 year old

College Station resident

 

Evading is a Class B Misdemeanor punishable by confinement in jail not to exceed 180 days and a fine not to exceed $2000.  Burglary of a habitation is a second degree felony punishable by imprisonment of not more than 20 years but not less than two years and a fine of up to $10,000.

 

The case has been turned over to the Criminal Investigations Division for further follow up.

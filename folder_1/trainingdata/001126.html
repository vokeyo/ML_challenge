If you are unable to find an MRC Unit in your preferred location, contact your local public health or emergency management leaders, or a nearby Citizen Corps Council. Citizen Corps Councils are often involved in the early formation of local MRC Units and can usually provide information on recent activities in their areas. Other sources of information include the State MRC Coordinators and the Regional MRC Coordinators.
